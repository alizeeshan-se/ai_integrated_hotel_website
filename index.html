Our Story

A Legacy of
Pashtun Hospitality

Nestled in the heart of Mardan, KPK, Rorjan Restaurant & Hotel has been a beacon of authentic Pashtun culture and hospitality for over fifteen years. Our name echoes the warmth of welcoming guests as family.

We blend traditional Peshawari flavors with modern comforts — creating an experience that honors our roots while embracing the finest standards of contemporary luxury.
